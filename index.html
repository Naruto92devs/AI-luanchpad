Whitepaper

Features

The platform can use AI to learn users' preferences and habits, providing a more personalized and user-friendly experience.

No More Gas Wars

The high gas fees in a pre-sale for an Initial DEX Offering (IDO) can be a result of high demand for participating in the sale and limited available slots, leading to competition among users and driving up the fees required to have a transaction processed by the network. To avoid this and build a fair system we will let AI algorithms predict who will be able to contibute in a presale.

Roadmap 2023

This roadmap assumes a target delivery in 8 weeks, with a demo launchpad ready in 2 weeks. It is important to note that the actual timeline may vary depending on the complexity of the launchpad and the resources available.

Week 1-2 :

Requirements gathering & planning

  • Define the goals and objectives of the launchpad
  • Identify key features and functionalities required to meet these objectives and launch a demo launchpad with entire UI and some functionality
  • Identify and prioritize the use cases for AI in the launchpad
  • Create a detailed project plan

Week 3-4 :

Technical architecture & infrastructure setup

  • Define the technical architecture and infrastructure required to support the launchpad
  • Set up the necessary hardware and software systems
  • Establish the development environment

Week 5-6 :

Development & integration of AI algorithms & technologies

  • Develop the AI algorithms and technologies required for the launchpad, such as NLP, ML, DL, RPA, and blockchain
  • Integrate the AI algorithms and technologies into the launchpad
  • Test and validate the AI algorithms and technologies to ensure they are working as expected

Week 7-8 :

User testing and refinement

  • Conduct user testing of the demo launchpad to validate the functionality and performance of the AI algorithms and technologies
  • Refine the launchpad based on user feedback
  • Prepare the launchpad for deployment
